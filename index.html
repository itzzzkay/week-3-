AKAN NAME GENERATOR

Description:

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. This web-based application generates your Akan name based on your date of birth and your gender.

Enter your date of birth and gender to generate your Akan name.
